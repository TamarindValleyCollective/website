WeCommunities develops sustainable residential farm communities across India — pairing eco-conscious housing with regenerative agriculture on large tracts of land, run on community-first decision-making about land stewardship and shared living.

TVC's founding group partnered with WeCommunities to turn a seed idea into an actual piece of land back in 2016 — TVC was their very first project, and the model that later grew into their current collectives.
