It is now 7 years since we started this journey so we thought we will invite volunteers to come see the transformation.

This year we are opening this event to 20 volunteers/enthusiasts who would like to come visit us and understand about the project, get some hands on experience on what it means to do farming.

Breakfast and Lunch is arranged at the farm with prior confirmation. Please carry a cap, sunglasses, water bottle and preferably a plate and glass.

If you have any saplings growing at home, please bring them along as well.
