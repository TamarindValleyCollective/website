Workshop

> Reva and Ranjan of **Primalise** have been living in an (almost) off-grid mud house - with no furniture and appliances. They are attempting to go back to being natives of nature.  
> To reconnect with the elements and mend the broken energy cycles. All this, while living in the city.

 Using examples, practical tips and thinking templates, the workshop helped the participants spot incongruence between who they are and the lifestyles they lead; and possible ways to find congruence.
